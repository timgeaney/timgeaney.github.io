beach hut's new website launched today

  • Posted in: design, responsive
  • Written by: Tim Geaney
  • On: 29th Febuary 2016

Today see's the launch of Beach Hut's new website. We wanted something distinctive, bold and colorful and a little different from most of the other online web deisgn websites and I think we have acheived that. I'm sure it will go through some iterations and tweaks in the coming weeks but we hope you like it.

A well designed website is now more important than ever for companies and individuals to succeed online.

A lot of work goes into building and branding a new website and sometimes you can lose track of your overall goals during the process but our goals have always been simple. We want to do great work for great clients.

A well designed website is now more important than ever for companies and individuals to succeed online. Consumers expectations for websites have increased significantly especially in recent years. Also the shift to mobile viewing with over 50% of all web traffic being viewed on mobile devices predicted by the end of this year, means mobile has never been more important.

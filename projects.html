    A learning management systems for Accounting College

    Accounting College are a UK Start Up that want to provide business and accoutancy courses online specifically tailored around the ACCA exams.

    They needed a website that would display their content in a clear and engaging way and since most of their content is video it was very important to them that the site display video well.

    They also needed to be able to promote and sell their courses. So we adapted a propeirtry Learning Management System around those requirements giving them a cost effective way of achieving their goals.

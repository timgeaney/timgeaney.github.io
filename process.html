process

“Always trust the process”
A typical web design project normally includes the following steps. However we adapt the process to suit the project in order to deliver you the best possible results.

Initial consultation

We’ll talk through your requirements and start exploring the options. This is the point where we define very specific goals for your project. This is leads on to research and information gathering.

Step 1

Quote

We’ll provide either a fixed quote or a high level estimate for the project (depending on how precisely the amount of work can be predicted at this stage) — for most projects, you will receive a fixed quote.

Step 2

Design and Development

Now its time to start building. You may need to provide any materials or information we need at this stage in order to carry out the work.We work iteratively, breaking the project into logical chunks. We'll test everything thoroughly at each stage to ensure everything works perfectly.

Step 3

Feedback and Alterations

We'll present the work on our test server for your review. Depending on the project size, this may be done at key milestones or at the end of the project. We'll listen to your feedback and carry out any further web design and development until you're completely happy.

We'll work closely with you to plan the launch. As well as the actual go-live, we'll do all the extras such as setting up Google Analytics, sitemaps, configuring redirects, setting up plugins for security and spam-prevention, etc.

Step 4

Training and Go-Live

If required, we'll also provide formal or informal training sessions for your new website to make sure you're up to speed with everything.

Step 5

Support

Once the project has gone live, we’ll provide 60 days' free support to make sure everything goes smoothly.

But it doesn't end there — all successful websites evolve over time. We'll work together to continue developing your website, ensuring it meets your needs over the longer-term.

Step 6
Lets Get Started
